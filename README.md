# Insect Perception — Interactive Atlas

An interactive web-based atlas of mathematical models of insect perception, neural processing, and bio-intelligent cyborg insect control.

**Author:** Chowdhury Mohammad Masum Refat
**Affiliation:** Morishima Lab, Department of Mechanical Engineering, The University of Osaka

---

## About

This interactive atlas visualizes 16 chapters of insect perception research, ranging from foundational neural models (Leaky Integrate-and-Fire, Izhikevich) through circuit-level architectures (olfaction, motion detection, ring attractors, mushroom body learning) to applied cyborg-insect control strategies.

Each chapter includes:
- An interactive 3D or schematic visualization
- Tunable parameters with real-time response
- Live signal plots
- Equations and reference citations

## Featured Research

The Bio-Intelligent Cyborg Insect (BCI), Insect Synergy Circuit (ISC), and Perception-Driven Control Strategy chapters are based on the following peer-reviewed publications from Morishima Lab:

- Refat, Ariyanto, Tanaka, Yamamoto & Morishima (2025). **Bio-Intelligent Cyborg Insect.** *Advanced Intelligent Systems.* DOI: [10.1002/aisy.202400838](https://doi.org/10.1002/aisy.202400838)

- Refat et al. (2026). **Perception-driven control strategy for bio-intelligent cyborg insect.** *ROBOMECH Journal*, 13:16. DOI: [10.1186/s40648-026-00344-7](https://doi.org/10.1186/s40648-026-00344-7)
